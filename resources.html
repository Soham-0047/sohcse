  • Focus on weak areas identified in mocks

From Toppers

💡 Pro Tips for GATE 2027

🎯

Aim for top 100, not just qualify

Aim high. If you target AIR 100, you'll likely land in top 1000. If you aim only to qualify, you might miss cutoff.

📝

PYQs > Everything

Solve last 15 years' PYQs at least 3 times. 70% of GATE questions are concept-repeats with new numbers.

📊

Mocks are non-negotiable

Take 30+ full mocks. Time management is what separates AIR 100 from AIR 1000.

🚫

Don't collect resources

Pick one textbook + one video lecture per subject. Finish them. Don't keep switching.

📓

Maintain short notes

Last 30 days, you'll only revise short notes. Start writing them from day 1.

😴

Sleep is study

7+ hours of sleep is non-negotiable. Burnout costs you months. Consistency > intensity.
