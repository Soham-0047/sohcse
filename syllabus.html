General Aptitude

The GATE CSE paper has 10 questions on General Aptitude (15 marks) and 55 questions on CSE subjects (85 marks). Question types: MCQ (single correct, negative marking), MSQ (multiple correct, no negative), NAT (numerical answer, no negative).
